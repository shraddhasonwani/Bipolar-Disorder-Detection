The Challenge

Up to 60% of Bipolar Disorder cases are initially misdiagnosed as Major Depressive Disorder, leading to inappropriate treatment and worsened outcomes. Diagnostic delays average **$\approx 6-10$ years**.

Our Solution

AI MULTIMODAL combines voice biomarkers, facial expressions, and conversational AI through a **Cross-Modal Attentional Network** and Temporal Fusion Layer to provide accurate, real-time diagnosis with clinical explanations.

Advanced AI Capabilities

Cutting-edge technology for clinical excellence

Multimodal Fusion

Simultaneous fusion of **Voice Biomarkers**, **Facial Action Units (AUs)**, and **Transformer-encoded linguistic features** for holistic assessment.

Real-Time Processing

Continuous monitoring and instant analysis with average response time of 2.3 seconds for in-session clinical support.

Explainable AI (XAI)

**SHAP-based Feature Attribution** provides transparent, clinician-interpretable reasoning, highlighting the *specific* cross-modal biomarkers driving the classification.

Treatment Planning

Evidence-based treatment recommendations with personalized medication and therapy protocols tailored by AI insights.

Risk Stratification

**Clinical Alert System:** Built-in protocols for identifying and flagging acute changes in state (e.g., suicide risk, manic episodes) via continuous monitoring.

Performance Tracking

Continuous monitoring of diagnostic accuracy and treatment effectiveness with detailed analytics for clinical audit.

The AI Engine: Technical Stack

A breakdown of the cutting-edge frameworks powering our diagnostic precision.

Core Language

Python for scientific computing and data pipeline orchestration.

NLP / Text

**BERT / RoBERTa (Transformers)** for contextual language embeddings and sentiment analysis.

Video / Facial

**OpenFace** for robust extraction of Action Unit (AU) intensities and Gaze vectors.

Audio / Voice

**OpenSMILE & eGeMAPS** feature sets for clinically validated voice biomarker extraction.

Model Architecture

**Temporal Fusion Network** with Cross-Modal Attention and Bi-LSTM layers.

Explainability

**SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations)** for post-hoc model interpretability and clinician trust.

Ethics, Privacy, and Clinical Oversight

Our commitment to responsible AI development in mental healthcare.

Data Privacy & Security

Full **HIPAA compliance** and end-to-end encryption. All patient data is anonymized and processed according to strict clinical governance standards. We adhere to **data minimization** principles.

Fairness & Bias Mitigation

Rigorous auditing of training data and model performance across demographic groups to actively identify and correct algorithmic bias, ensuring **equitable clinical recommendations**.

Human Oversight & Consent

The AI is a **clinical support tool**, not a replacement. All diagnoses require final confirmation by a licensed clinician. Transparent **informed consent** is mandatory before any data collection.
